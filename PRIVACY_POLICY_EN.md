## 2. General Information

The protection of your personal data is very important to us. This Privacy Policy informs you about what data we collect in the "Manga Sammlung" app, for what purpose, and how we process it.

The use of our app involves the processing of personal data. This Privacy Policy informs you in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) about the type, scope, and purpose of the processing of personal data.

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account Data

Registration is required to use the app. We collect:

- **Email address** (required)
- **Name** (required)
- **Password** (stored encrypted)

**Legal Basis:** Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance)
**Purpose:** Creation and management of your user account, authentication

### 3.2 Manga Collection Data

As part of using the app, we store the following data about your manga collection:

- Manga title and volume number
- Author, genre, publisher
- ISBN number
- Language
- Reading status (read, unread, duplicate, buy new)
- Cover image URLs
- Notes and ratings
- Timestamps (creation and modification date)

**Legal Basis:** Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance)
**Purpose:** Provision of the app's core functionality (managing your manga collection)

### 3.3 Technical Data

When using the app, the following technical data is automatically collected:

- IP address (temporarily for API requests)
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Timestamps of API requests
- Error logs (crash logs)

**Legal Basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest)
**Purpose:** Ensuring technical functionality, error analysis, security

### 3.4 Firebase Push Notifications

For push notifications, we use Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google). The following data is processed:

- Firebase Device Token
- Notification timestamps
- Delivery status

**Legal Basis:** Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)
**Purpose:** Sending notifications about new manga releases or app updates

You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

## 4. Camera Access

The app requires access to your camera exclusively for the ISBN scanner feature. The captured images are:

- **NOT stored**
- **NOT transmitted to servers**
- Only temporarily processed in memory for barcode recognition
- Immediately deleted after processing

**Legal Basis:** Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)
**Purpose:** Enabling ISBN barcode scanning

## 5. Data Sharing and Recipients

### 5.1 Our Own Servers

Your data is stored and processed on our own servers (manga-api.phudevelopement.xyz). The server is located in Germany (Koblenz, Rhineland-Palatinate) and is self-hosted.

### 5.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

**Firebase (Google LLC)**
- Purpose: Push notifications
- Location: USA (with EU-US Data Privacy Framework)
- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### 5.3 No Sharing with Third Parties

We do **NOT** share your personal data with third parties, except:
- You have expressly consented (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
- There is a legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)
- The sharing is necessary for contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)

## 6. Data Storage and Deletion

### 6.1 Storage Duration

- **Account data:** Until deletion of your account
- **Manga collection data:** Until manual deletion by you or account deletion
- **Technical logs:** Maximum 30 days
- **Crash reports:** Maximum 90 days

### 6.2 Deletion

You can at any time:
- Delete individual manga entries
- Delete your entire account (in app settings or via email request)

After account deletion, all your data will be completely and irrevocably deleted within 30 days.

## 7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption:** HTTPS/TLS for all data transmissions
- **Password hashing:** Passwords are stored encrypted with bcrypt
- **Secure tokens:** JWT tokens for authentication
- **Access control:** Strict access restrictions on servers and database
- **Regular updates:** Security updates for servers and app
